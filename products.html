Espresso

Breville Barista Espresso Machine

The Breville Barista Express is the perfect espresso machine for beginners to advanced baristas. The machine has the capability to be fully automated, or can be manual for those baristas wishing to experiment with different espresso variables. The Barista Express comes with a portafilter and four baskets, two single and two double shots. The dual walls of two of the baskets garauntees a perfect shot every time. A steamer wand with milk pitcher are included as well as a built in grinder with grind size variability.

Open Bottom Portafilter

The open bottom portafilter, besides being asthetically pleasing, can aid in the perfection of your shot.

Needle Distribution Tool

The needle distribution tool can assist in tamping the perfect espresso puck. the fine needles are prefect for dispersing the coffee grinds evenly and breaking up unwanted clumps.

Tamper and Distributor

The tamper distributor tools is a great addition to any barista attempting to master the art of the perfect shot. One side helps event distribute the beans in a smooth layer, while the other allows for a firm press of the coffee beans to ensure the puck is tamped to perfection.
